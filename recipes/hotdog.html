Boiled Lasagna

Description

Taste the heavenly Syrian Hot-Dog by tasting the brand new but time-tested XXI century recipe created in Chuchmekistan!

Easy recipe that combines the world cuisines in the most unique manner.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Hot Dog Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Tomato Ketchup

Steps

  1. Take one bread and spread peanut butter on it. The more the better.
  2. Take another bread and spread ketchup on it. The more the better.
  3. Peel a banana and put it between the two hot-dog breads.
  4. Best enjoyed with a warm glass of carbonated donkey milk!
