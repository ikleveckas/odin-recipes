Carbonated Milk

Description

New type of carbonated milk was invented in Karakalpakstan.

Like coke, but with milk!

Ingredients

  • Milk - best results with donkey/horse milk, but cow milk is OK too.
  • CO2.
  • Yeast.

Steps

  1. Add milk into a container
  2. Add yeast to milk.
  3. Let milk sit for a few days in warmth.
  4. Add CO2 into the milk with a carbonator device for extra bubbles.
  5. Enjoy with meat dishes.
